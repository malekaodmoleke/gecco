The Early Childhood Education and Care Sustainability Forum

Founded in March 2021, the forum provides a platform for members of the ECEC sector committed to increasing sustainability, covering all three pillars plus governance.

The aim of the forum

To support the development of a holistic and transformational Early Childhood Education and Care sector that promotes lifelong learning and empowers the whole community of children, families, professional educators and administrators.

The forum encourages informed decisions and responsible actions for environmental integrity, economic viability and a just society for present and future generations.

The group intends to achieve this by:

  • Sharing experiences and knowledge in a supportive manner.
  • Sharing best practice with others to role model and support sustainability.
  • Educating practitioners from the bottom up.
  • Engaging CEOs, nursery owners, local authorities, parents and politicians.
  • Challenging greenwash and publicising practical evidence-based research.

Membership

Membership is open to anyone in the sector with an interest in supporting, encouraging and role-modelling sustainability in the ECEC sector. There are no restrictions on numbers.

Members meet every six weeks via Zoom, and non-members may be invited to group meetings to speak and share experiences or knowledge.
