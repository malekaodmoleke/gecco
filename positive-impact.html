People First

Positive Impact on People

Supporting children, parents, colleagues, learners and the wider community.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to provide care and education to children and to support their parents. These impacts are measured through our KPIs and aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

We aim for all children to achieve good levels of development, while also providing meaningful careers for colleagues and learners.

We promote the health and well-being of colleagues and volunteers through inclusive policies, support initiatives, practical benefits and a culture of care.

Protecting People

We ensure that we:

  • Protect the health and safety of employees and the community.
  • Avoid harming the lives of local people.
  • Support diversity and inclusion.
  • Take economic responsibility.
  • Maintain risk registers and access statements for each building.

Volunteering

The company welcomes volunteers from schools, young people, adults exploring childcare careers, and community partners such as churches, hospitals and care homes.

Colleagues are supported to volunteer through flexible time arrangements, while the company contributes to social impact through fundraising, food banks, shared resources and recycling collections.

Human Rights

We are a committed equal opportunity employer and abide by fair labour practices. We ensure that our activities do not directly or indirectly violate human rights in any country, including forced labour.

We engage in gender pay reporting and work to reduce any gaps identified.

Donations and Aid

The company allocates 1% of EBITDA to each of its two core charities: GECCO and the Employee Trust. Donations focus on alleviating need, supporting sustainability education and building community partnerships.

Supporting the Community

The company prioritises the health and safety of employees, learners and the wider community, maintaining risk registers and access statements for each building and department.

Transparency

The organisation is committed to honesty and openness in all communications, requiring colleagues to report truthfully in sustainability reports, financial reports, questionnaires and press releases.

Human Rights and Diversity

We ensure compliance with fair labour practices, gender pay equality, diversity, inclusion and avoidance of human rights violations.

Learning and Educating

The company invests in professional development, training and research projects to improve operations and reduce environmental impact.

  • Tracking child development and staff progression.
  • Conducting training needs analyses.
  • Assessing financial impacts on staff.

Environmental Protection

The company protects the environment through sustainable purchasing, reducing single-use plastics, recycling, conserving energy, renewable technologies and minimising fossil fuel use.

Sustainable Transport Policy

Every location has a green transport policy relevant to that location, including public transport routes, advice for walkers and cyclists, car sharing opportunities and moving to electric vehicles.

Community Engagement

The company supports colleagues’ charitable efforts and provides engagement through local initiatives, cultural celebrations, environmental campaigns and company-led programmes.
